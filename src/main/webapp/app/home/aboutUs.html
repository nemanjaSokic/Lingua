We are young team from Novi Sad located in Serbia. Our mision is to present product Lingua. program is intendent for professors and students. It's very simple for use.

If you are professor, you can create course for determined langages. Select and add/delete determined student from it. Professors have special dashboard where can push some simple or assigment for students. In progress is other functionality and practicality.

If you are student you can see on dashboard news about next class. You can send private messages to professors. You can dowload preliminar test who will tell you what level you are and whic group is adequate for you.
